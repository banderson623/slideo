The Pauline Cycle.  The mission of the church is simple: evangelize strategic cities, establish churches, and entrust those churches to well trained leaders.

Paul’s Establishing Process

Phase 1: He proclaimed the gospel.

Phase 2: He gathered the Christians into a community, strengthened them in their faith, and appointed elders in every church to oversee the community.

Phase 3: He continued the process of establishing the churches both by letters and by visits and by training faithful men to assist him in the establishing process.

Phase 4: He used the churches as a base for taking the gospel to new frontiers and encouraged the churches to participate with him in the furtherance of the gospel.

Phase 5: He charged the key men he had trained to assist him in fully establishing the churches and to find other faithful men that they could train to carry on this task for the next generation.
